What is Technology


Technology is combination of knowledge, tools, arts and science masterfully combined it ranged from the ancient age up to our modern times, which began when early human species started to convert natural resources into simple tools. Over time they started to use fire for everyday use, invented wheels for transporting small to large objects, developed medicine to treat different kinds of disease, humans even managed to create a machine that enable them to navigate through air and water.
